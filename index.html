Research

My research interests lie in the area of natural language processing as follows: Large Language Models, Factual Inconsistency, Multimodal, Knowledge Augmentation, Information Retrieval, etc.

Recently, I am passionated to (1) mitigate the hallucination of Large Language Models by leveraging feedback; (2) store and utilize the knowledge as the form of Pluggable Knowledge Memory.
